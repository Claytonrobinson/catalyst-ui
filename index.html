Sustainable Aviation Fuel from Energy Crops & Agricultural Waste

Project Overview

This senior design project develops AI-assisted catalytic systems for converting cellulosic biomass (switchgrass, miscanthus, poplar, agricultural wastes) into sustainable aviation fuel (SAF). The two-step process uses dilute acid hydrolysis followed by multi-reaction catalysis (hydrolysis, dehydration, hydrogenation, deoxygenation) with Brønsted/Lewis acid + Ni-based metal catalysts on ZrO₂ support. AI/ML (screening + Bayesian optimization) accelerates catalyst design to boost yields, >C10 fraction, and heating value. Novelty: Fast-tracked development of non-noble polymetallic catalysts in low-energy aqueous conditions.
