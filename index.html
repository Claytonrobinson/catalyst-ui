Converting Energy Crops & Agricultural Waste → Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF)

Catalyst Dashboard Overview

Purpose: This dashboard demonstrates AI/ML-assisted catalyst screening & optimization for producing sustainable aviation fuel (SAF) from cellulosic biomass (energy crops like switchgrass, miscanthus, poplar + agricultural waste). The two-step process: 1) Dilute acid hydrolysis in hot compressed water → soluble oligosaccharides. 2) Catalytic upgrading (hydrolysis, dehydration, hydrogenation, deoxygenation) using ZrO₂-supported WO₃ with Ni + secondary metal promoter. AI accelerates metal selection, synthesis params, and Bayesian optimization for higher yields, >C10 fraction, and heating value. Novelty: Fast, low-cost, aqueous, non-noble catalyst development.

Images: Energy crops (switchgrass/miscanthus) and SAF production concepts – real feedstocks for the catalyst.
